Wedding of Kačí & Robi

22nd August 2026

Sibřina, Prague-East

Map

The wedding will take place at Spolek Sosak, in the village of Sibřina, Prague-East district.

About the Celebration

We are planning a day full of joy, great food, and shared memories. Our wedding celebration will take place in the beautiful surroundings of Sibřina, where we can all come together and mark this milestone.

We hope to enjoy free time, music, and a relaxed atmosphere close to Prague. We look forward to having you with us.

Practical Information

🚗

Getting There

Parking on site will be provided. We will not be organising group transport, as guests will be arriving from different directions. If you have trouble getting here, please let us know and we will be happy to help.

For the journey home in the evening we recommend Uber, Bolt, or a taxi. We are happy to help you call one.

🗺️

The Venue

The wedding will take place mostly outdoors — there is plenty of open space. We will have marquees and a large tent ready in case of rain. We still recommend bringing a spare pair of warmer, rain-proof shoes just in case.

🏠

Accommodation

We are unfortunately unable to arrange quality accommodation for everyone, but the following will be available on site:

  • Space for a tent in the garden (plenty of room)
  • A shared sleeping area in the loft — a large communal space; we recommend bringing your own sleeping mat and sleeping bag. Several bathrooms and toilets are available.
👨‍👧

Who to Bring

  • Children are very welcome! We will have our two little ones with us too. There will be plenty of space and activities for kids!
  • Please leave four-legged friends at home.
🎒

What to Bring

  • Good spirits!
  • A spare, warmer outfit and rain-proof shoes.
  • Anything fun — homemade spirits, musical instruments, home-baked goods, etc.
